## 👋 About

Hi! I'm **Saloni Mathur**, a designer at **Cinema Club, IIT Roorkee**.

As a part of the design team, I work on creating visual content that represents the club across social media. My work primarily focuses on designing Instagram posts and stories that are informative, aesthetically engaging, and aligned with the club's branding.

From movie recommendations and educational carousels to event announcements and themed content, every design is created with an emphasis on storytelling, typography, composition, and audience engagement.
